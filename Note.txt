| CUDA            | nvcc traffic_cuda.cu -o traffic_cuda                         | ./traffic_cuda                                          |
| Hybrid MPI+OpenMP | mpicc -fopenmp traffic_hybrid_mpi_openmp.c -o traffic_hybrid | export OMP_NUM_THREADS=4; mpirun -np 2 ./traffic_hybrid |
